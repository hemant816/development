Similarly, Members will only be obligated to comply with requests to the extent legally required, but may choose to comply with other requests in their sole discretion.

Access and correct your Personal Information

Much of your Personal Information is available in your DocMode.org account. Your name, email address, and other identifiable profile information is editable through the profile settings in your DocMode.org account except as noted below. Your account dashboard lists all current and archived courses in which you have enrolled and includes links to any Verified Certificates you may have earned. The dashboard also contains copies of your answers and other participation and performance in courses. Please contact DocMode.org learner support to access and update this or other information. If your request involves information controlled by a Member, DocMode.org learner support will notify the appropriate Member of your request.

Exceptions:

  • Usernames cannot ever be changed. This is an DocMode.org system restriction. Because usernames identify you in courses and elsewhere on the DocMode.org Site, DocMode.org encourages you to use discretion in choosing your username. If you want to protect your identity, do not use your real name or a name identifiable to you as your username.
  • DocMode.org does not track or index every time or every place on the DocMode.org Site when or where a learner may volunteer Personal Information, so neither DocMode.org nor Members will be able to help you locate or manage all such instances. DocMode.org encourages you to use discretion before volunteering Personal Information on the DocMode.org Site.

Restrict or object to the processing of Personal Information

If you want to manage emails and other communications to you, you may update your preferences in your DocMode.org account dashboard, follow the steps described in links at the bottom of email messages, or contact DocMode.org learner support. You may object to, or request that DocMode.org or a Member stop, its use of your Personal Information for other purposes by contacting the DocMode.org learner support team. If your request involves information controlled by a Member, DocMode.org learner support will notify the appropriate Member of your request. Please note that if you choose to restrict DocMode.org’s or a Member’s ability to process Personal Information, and the processing is otherwise required to provide you with various services and products offered on the DocMode.org Site, you may not be able to establish an DocMode.org user account or enroll for a course, and DocMode.org or a Member may not be able to provide you with those services or products. Delete account and Personal Information

To request deletion of your DocMode.org account and Personal Information, you should click the button labeled “Delete my account” in your DocMode.org account settings. Because deletion on the DocMode.org system is a permanent action and cannot be reversed, DocMode.org may ask you to complete a process that aims to confirm your authority to manage the DocMode.org account affected by your request.

With respect to your DocMode.org account, DocMode.org will permanently:

  • deactivate your DocMode.org account,
  • remove the active profile,
  • retire your username, and
  • remove you from DocMode.org email lists.

With respect to your Personal Information, DocMode.org will permanently delete your DocMode.org account profile Personal Information from the DocMode.org Site.

As noted above, to the extent that DocMode.org associates the information that DocMode.org collects directly or indirectly with an individual (for example, you), the association is based on Personal Information in your DocMode.org account profile. By erasing the Personal Information in your DocMode.org account profile, the remaining information about your activity on the DocMode.org Site will no longer be associated with you, except as noted below.

These changes will be applied to data stores that are used for operation of the DocMode.org Site including course administration by Members. If you enrolled in any courses on the DocMode.org Site, the DocMode.org learner support team will share your request with the Members that offered those courses.

Exceptions:
